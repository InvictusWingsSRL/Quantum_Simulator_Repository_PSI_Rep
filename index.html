The Repository: This repository regroups the existing quantum computer simulators. The classification is made according to a wide-enough set of criteria in order to guide your selection. This taxonomy is the result of our research work and analysis. So, if you have benefited somehow from our repository, please make sure to cite our work to aknowledge our efforts.
  • Plain citation: Zakaria Abdelmoiz Dahi and Enrique Alba and Rodrigo Gil-Merino and Francisco Chicano and and Gabriel Luque. A Survey on Quantum Computer Simulators. Proceedings of the 19th Conference of the Spanish Association for Artificial Intelligence, CAEPIA. 941-946
